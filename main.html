Forklife

Platformer

Forklife started as an idea in my head for a game about forklifts and cargo shipping. Then when I got the opportunity to choose a game project I chose to make Forklife a reality as my first ever self-made game. Forklife allowed me to engage and involve myself within Unity and actually learn how to make a game from scratch.

The assets I used are self made basic cubes and the textures are from textureheaven.com.

Deck of 2020

Card Game

Deck of 2020 is a card game made for the annual GMTK Game Jam of 2020. Which I was in a team with one other person. I had responsibility of how the cards looked and their behaviour.

The assets in-game are self made. This game is also fully modular so the player can put in other cards they want to play with.
